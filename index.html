A Leader in Exploration, Production, and Operations

Spooner Petroleum Company is an independent company focused on exploration, production and operations of oil and gas properties in Mississippi, Alabama and Louisiana. With almost 40 years of experience in the area, Spooner Petroleum has built a strong reputation for knowledge and integrity along with an extensive geologic database of the region.

Contract Operations Service

Spooner Petroleum has become the leading expert in the area for deep, high pressure, sour gas well drilling, completion, and production. The company also has experience in the design, development, drilling, and completion of salt dome gas storage and high volume saltwater disposal wells. Spooner Petroleum offers it's expertise as a contract service to area oil and gas producers. >>

Exploration and Investment

Spooner Petroleum Company's exploration efforts resulted in new field or new pool discoveries in Alabama, Mississippi, and Alabama. In addition to continuing it's own exploration efforts, Spooner Petroleum offers contract development geology, joint interest billing, and revenue distribution services to other oil and gas producers.
